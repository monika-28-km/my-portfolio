A comprehensive web-based system designed to manage faculty information efficiently. Smart City Issue Tracker

A civic engagement platform that enables citizens to report and track urban issues in their city. Users can submit complaints about problems like potholes, broken streetlights, garbage collection, or water supply. Each report includes GPS location, photos, and description. The system assigns reports to departments, tracks resolution progress, and sends status notifications to users.

React Node.js MongoDB Google Maps API
